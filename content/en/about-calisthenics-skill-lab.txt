What started as a personal passion evolved into my full-time mission in April 2023 when I founded Calisthenics Zaanstad. After years of building a thriving community there, my move to Amsterdam in February 2026 marked the beginning of a new chapter: <strong>Calisthenics SkillLab Amsterdam</strong>.

section_title: Where Community Meets Mastery
section_image: images/a_new_vision_for_amsterdam.jpeg
paragraph: Building on the foundation of Calisthenics Zaanstad, the Amsterdam SkillLab is designed for those who want to combine a high-energy community with elite-level coaching.
paragraph: In Amsterdam, we offer the best of both worlds. You can join our vibrant group classes to work on your general fitness and skills alongside others, or choose our Private and Semi-Private sessions for a more targeted and personalized experience. This approach allows us to provide every athlete with the right level of guidance—whether you’re thriving on the energy of the group or looking for a professional, tailor-made plan to unlock specific high-level skills. At the SkillLab, your progress is tracked with precision, ensuring you grow effectively at your own pace.
heading2: The Best Training Grounds in the City
paragraph: We take our training to the most inspiring outdoor spots in Amsterdam. Whether we are working on your skills in Rembrandtpark or Oosterpark, or hitting the calisthenics park near Sloterplas for our Semi-Private sessions & grouplessons , the city is our gym.
paragraph: My goal is to help you build more than just muscle. We focus on:
bullet: Mastering Impressive Skills | Handstands, Muscle-ups, and Levers
bullet: Improving Posture & Mobility | Move better, feel better
bullet: Building Confidence | Balance, coordination, and self-belief
paragraph: Whether you are a complete beginner or an advanced athlete looking for that competitive edge, I am here to provide the structure and coaching you need to reach your goals.
